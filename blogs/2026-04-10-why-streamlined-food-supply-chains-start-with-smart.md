When it comes to global supply chain transparency and regulatory compliance, Certificates of Analysis (COAs) serve the same function as a set of keys, unlocking movement at every node of the supply chain. But the headaches that manual COA review can cause are practically as standard to the industry as the documents themselves; it's a process that can be notoriously tedious and slow. We conducted a quick poll on LinkedIn that confirmed this reality; 80% of the professionals who responded identified manual document review as the single biggest bottleneck in their COA management process .
