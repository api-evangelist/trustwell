Updated: February 2026 Since Oct. 1, 2021, all prepacked for direct sale (PPDS) foods in the UK must include on the product packaging a full list of ingredients with any allergens emphasised (in bold, italics, a different font style, or colour, etc.). For Example: Pears, Peaches, Grapes, Apples, Bananas, Whipped Topping ( milk, soy ), Walnuts .
