For food and beverage companies, labeling accuracy connects directly to compliance, operational efficiency, and consumer safety. A single missed allergen, an outdated ingredient statement, or an incorrect date can trigger a costly product recall. These events disrupt production schedule, weaken trust with customers , and attracts the wrong kind of attention from regulators.
